Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We want you to love your keepsake. Every order is backed by our money-back guarantee, so ordering is risk-free.

Our money-back guarantee

If your enhanced image doesn't melt your heart, tell us and we'll refund you in full. No awkward emails, no fuss. Just get in touch and we'll put it right or give your money back.

How to request a refund

Email hello@scantobaby.co.uk with the email address you ordered with and a quick note about what wasn't right. We aim to reply within one working day.

When to ask

Please contact us within 14 days of receiving your enhanced image. Because every keepsake is made individually from your own scan, letting us know quickly helps us fix things or refund you fast.

Refund timing

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or card provider, it can take a few working days for the money to appear.

Printed and framed orders

If you ordered a printed and framed copy and it arrives damaged or faulty, send us a photo and we'll replace it or refund it. For returns of undamaged prints, get in touch and we'll talk you through the options.

Your statutory rights

This guarantee sits on top of, and does not affect, your legal rights as a consumer under UK law.
