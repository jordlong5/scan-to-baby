Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what personal information Scan to Baby collects, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have over it. We keep this deliberately plain. If anything is unclear, email us at hello@scantobaby.co.uk.

Who we are

Scan to Baby is a service operated by Scan to Baby Studio Ltd, a company registered in England & Wales. We are the data controller for the information described here. You can reach us at hello@scantobaby.co.uk.

What we collect

  • The images you send us — the 3D baby scan or ultrasound you upload so we can enhance it.
  • Contact details — your name and email address, so we can deliver your order and reply to you.
  • Order details — what you bought and any notes or gift messages you add.
  • Payment information — card and billing details are entered directly into our payment provider's secure checkout. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Basic technical data — standard information your browser sends, and anonymous analytics about how the site is used.

How we use it

  • To create your enhanced keepsake image and deliver your order.
  • To take payment and send order confirmations and updates.
  • To answer your questions and provide support.
  • To meet our legal and accounting obligations.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your scan images to train public AI models or share them with anyone outside the fulfilment of your order.

Who we share it with

We only share data with the service providers we need to run the business, for example our payment processor and our website host. These providers act on our instructions and are bound to protect your data. We may also disclose information if required to do so by law.

How long we keep it

We keep your scan images and order information only as long as needed to deliver your order and meet our legal obligations. You can ask us to delete your images at any time once your order is complete, and we will do so promptly.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to have it corrected or deleted, to object to or restrict how we use it, and to request a copy of it. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@scantobaby.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies to make the site work and to understand how it is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top shows when it was last revised.
