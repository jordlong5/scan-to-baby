Inside the process

Not a filter. A full reconstruction pipeline.

Every scan, whether it's a 3D scan or a standard ultrasound, runs through a multi-stage enhancement process, part advanced computing, part trained human eye, so the face that comes back is sharper, more detailed and unmistakably yours.

Intake

Scan intake & calibration

We map the detail in your scan, 3D or ultrasound, and prepare it, cleaning up the soft edges and noise that come straight off the scanner.

Reconstruct

Neural reconstruction

Advanced imaging models run multiple enhancement passes on high-performance hardware, sharpening depth, contours and soft features from your 3D scan data.

Review

Specialist review

A trained imaging specialist refines every single image by hand. No order is ever sent without a human eye on it.

Deliver

Final render & delivery

Your finished image is rendered in high resolution, ready as a digital file and, if you choose, a gallery-quality framed print.
